This is NFL cheerleader Erica

Her tutorial is as follows:

  1. Wash hair with Pantene Pro-V volumizer shampoo
  2. Comb through hair with a wide tooth comb while shampoo is still in hair.
  3. Rinse and follow with Mixed Chicks leave in conditioner. Mixed Chicks wash out conditioner doesn't' work for me.
  4. After the conditioner is in, and while hair is still wet, I put LaBella styling gel (the pink gel)
  5. I then put heat protection gel on the hair and blow dry on hot for about 10 minutes and let the rest air dry.

This is Chantel

Her tutorial is as follows:

I cowashed/detangled my hair with Aussie Moist condish. Next, I placed some Vo5 condish on each section of hair and sealed with shea butter. Then I plopped with a towel for about 5 minutes. Took the towel off, shook my head a few times, and voila! Perfect Wash n Go.
